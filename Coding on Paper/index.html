Egg in a Hole

Ingredients

  • 1 slice of your choice of bread
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tbsp of butter
  • salt and pepper

Recipe

  1. Gather your materials! Your ingredients include bread, eggs and butter. You should use a pan, spatula and your stovetop.
  2. Cut out a hole in the middle of your bread. It doesn't have to be perfect, just something to put your egg in.
  3. Butter your bread, creating a thin layer that covers the bread and the cutout intil the edges. Make sure to butter both sides!
  4. Heat up your pan and start frying your bread and cutout until golden on the first side.
  5. Flip both and crack your egg into the hole. Lower the heat to medium and let the egg cook until you can flip it over again.
  6. Set your fully done cutout on your plate and continue to flip your bread until you're satisfied with the colour and how well your egg is cooked.
  7. Season with salt and pepper, and any other spices of your choice. A little tomato sauce on the side never hurt anyone ;)
  8. Enjoy!
