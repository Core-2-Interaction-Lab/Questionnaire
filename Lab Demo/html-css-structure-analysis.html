Wordle 

The div.MomentSystem-module_moment holds all of the content of the wordle page. 
There are two main sections within the body section, and those are the ad container, 
and game module. Within the game module are the header and game board. 

Housed in the game container are the game board and the keyboard. Within 
each of these are the tiles that make up the game, and the keyboard. 

The header houses 4 buttons. On the left is a button  with id="AppHeader-module_navButton__nKv2h", 
which reveals other games on the New York Times website, and on the right is three buttons with a 
div class="AppHeader-module_menuRight__Noasd". These three buttons have ids of “help-button”, 
“statistics-button” and “settings-button”  Also housed in the header is the Wordle Title, with 
class="AppHeader-module_title__EQr6V"
