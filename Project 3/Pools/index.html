Welcome to the "Pool by Shape" page, where a unique approach has been taken to exploring New York City's public pools. Using the polygon coordinates provided in the NYC parks pool data set, funky polygon shapes have been created for each public pool in the city. Please note that these shapes are not to scale and are intended for visual purposes only with the use of Chat GPT, SVG code has been generated to bring these shapes to life. While every effort has been made to ensure the accuracy of these shapes, please keep in mind that they may not be entirely precise due to the nature of the AI process. Nevertheless, they're a fun and distinctive way to explore the city's public pool system. So, take a plunge and explore the fascinating shapes of New York City's public pools!
